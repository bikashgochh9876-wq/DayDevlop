Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2026. Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data.

1. Introduction

DayDevlop ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website DayDevlop.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or services. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Contact Form: Name, email address, phone number, service type, and message
  • WhatsApp: Phone number and chat history when you contact us
  • Project Files: Content, images, logos, and branding materials you share for website development
  • Payment Info: Transaction details via UPI/Bank Transfer (we don't store card details)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • Processing your project and delivering website services
  • Sending project updates, invoices, and delivery notifications
  • Improving our services and website experience
  • Showcasing completed projects in portfolio (with permission)

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in these cases:

  • Service Providers: Hosting platforms (Netlify, Vercel) to deploy your website
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or government authorities
  • Business Transfer: If DayDevlop is acquired or merged with another company

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies for:

  • Essential cookies for website functionality
  • Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure storage of project files and client data
  • Limited access - only authorized team members can access data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Project files and client data are stored for 12 months after delivery for support purposes. You can request deletion anytime by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request your data in portable format

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (WhatsApp, Instagram, hosting platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
