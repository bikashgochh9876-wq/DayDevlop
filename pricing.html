Pricing FAQs

Common questions about our pricing and process

Do you charge monthly fees?

No. All prices are one-time only. You own the source code. Hosting is separate - you can use free hosting like Netlify/Vercel or buy your own hosting.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, Bank Transfer, or Cash. 50% advance to start, 50% on delivery. We share payment details on WhatsApp after finalizing requirements.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes! Start with any package and upgrade anytime. You only pay the difference between packages.

What if I need more revisions?

Each package includes 2-3 free revisions. Additional revisions are ₹500 per round. Most clients are happy within 2 rounds.
